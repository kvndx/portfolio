Hello!

I'm Kevin, a freelance graphic designer and creative from Los Angeles, California. I'm also currently a first-year Urban Studies and Planning and Political Science double-major at the University of California, San Diego.

I'm of the firm belief that we are all connected through the uncertainty we experience in the everyday with regards to ourselves, our lives, and the modern world - that we can all take solace and learn from our inherent fear of the unknown.

Driven by a desire to visualize our constant personal evolutions by way of these certain uncertainties, I have allowed my artistic whims to guide me through the creative process, primarily within the mediums of graphic design, analog and digital collage, and photography.

All things considered, I adore the act of artistic creation and what it entails and allows me to do. I utilize a wide range of visual mediums and experiment with various styles on every piece with a single goal in mind: to express myself to the best of my ability.

Process

Step 1

Gather physical materials and tools or launch creative program of choice.

Step 2

Begin wireframing or brainstorming different ideas for project.

Step 3

Experiment with different ephemera, sources, or assets to bring brainstormed ideas to life.

Step 4

Create many iterations and deviations of and from project using gathered materials.

Step 5

Rinse and repeat until project appears complete. Apply finishing touches and publish.
